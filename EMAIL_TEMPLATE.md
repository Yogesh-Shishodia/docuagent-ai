## Tips for Cold Outreach

1. **Personalize the first sentence** — mention something specific about their company or role
2. **Lead with their problem, not your solution** — "You can't use ChatGPT with your documents" beats "We built a cool on-prem RAG"
3. **Be specific about your use case** — "law firms handling HIPAA data" > "enterprises that need privacy"
4. **Always include a call-to-action** — ask for 20 minutes, offer a demo, request feedback
5. **Include a link** — GitHub (source code), demo video (if available), or a scheduling link
6. **Follow up once** — if no response in 7 days, send one follow-up (don't spam)
7. **Track what works** — measure open rates, response rates by template; iterate
